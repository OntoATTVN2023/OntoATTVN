Introduction to ATTVN Defense

The ATTVN Defense is crafted to provide a structured methodology for comprehending and implementing defensive countermeasures against cyber threats. It serves as a complement to the well-established MITRE ATT&CK framework, which delineates adversarial Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs).

While ATT&CK maps out the adversarial tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) within a framework, ATTVN Defense offers guidance on defending against those specific adversarial actions.

Understanding both the attack and defense is key to building effective cybersecurity.

The ATTVN Defense utilizes MITRE D3FEND and MITRE ATT&CK data to offer users a centralized platform for searching and learning about defense. This information enhances users' comprehension of attack techniques and effective defense strategies.
